At Mountain View, we embrace a diverse array of community styles designed to cater to the varying needs and preferences of our residents, all grounded in a human-centric approach. Our offerings include vibrant city living environments that provide dynamic urban experiences, parks living communities that blend nature and modern amenities for a tranquil lifestyle, signature living spaces that offer luxurious and exclusive residences, and coastal living areas that bring the serenity of the seaside to everyday life. Each community style is thoughtfully crafted with the well-being and happiness of our residents in mind, ensuring that every individual can find a home that resonates with their personal lifestyle and aspirations. By focusing on the unique needs of our diverse clientele, Mountain View creates harmonious and fulfilling living experiences that prioritize human connection and satisfaction.

Since its inception in 2005, Mountain View Developments has emerged as a pioneer in the Egyptian real estate market, recognized for creating high-quality living spaces that resonate with a deep sense of belonging. As Egypt’s first and only experience focused real estate developer, Mountain View is dedicated to crafting communities where happiness and a sense of home are paramount. With a diverse portfolio of 20 residential projects and ongoing developments in West Cairo and now in Saudi Arabia, Mountain View continues to lead the way in innovation. Their unwavering commitment to quality and novelty has cemented their reputation as a trusted industry leader. At Mountain View, the mission is clear: to create vibrant spaces where people can live, work, and experience happiness. Driven by innovation, Mountain View for development and real estate investment is among Egypt’s leading private property development companies. Specialized in developing first tier residences and resorts, Mountain View boasts over ten projects across Egypt, in some of the country’s most prime locations, including East and West Cairo, the Red Sea Coast and the North Coast.
